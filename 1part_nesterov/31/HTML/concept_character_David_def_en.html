Doctor David Marcus was a male Human United Federation of Planets scientist who lived during the mid-to-late 23rd century.Born in 2261 to Dr. Carol Marcus and Starfleet officer, James T. Kirk. He grew up in a single-parent environment with his mother, immersed in a world of scientific research. David knew of Kirk, but never met him, because his mother effectively banned Kirk from his life; she felt they lived in two different worlds, and she wanted David in hers.
