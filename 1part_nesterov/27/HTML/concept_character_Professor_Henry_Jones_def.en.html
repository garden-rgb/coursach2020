Was a professor of medieval literature, expert on Grail lore, the father of archaeologist Indiana Jones, and the husband of Anna Mary Jones. Jones had a difficult relationship with his son which worsened following the untimely death of Anna in 1912. The two drifted further apart but when the Joneses were reunited during their search for the Holy Grail years later, near-death experiences for the both of them undid their estrangement and strengthened the connection between father and son
